/FAQ

Simple by design.

What is Halo Revival?

A planning app for aesthetic goals, surgeon consult discovery, medspa research, saved providers, reference notes, and booking prep.

Is it only for surgery?

No. Surgical consults and non-surgical maintenance each get dedicated flows so the research stays easier to compare.

Does it replace medical advice?

No. Halo Revival is for organization and education. Treatment decisions should come from licensed clinicians.
