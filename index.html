FAQ

Simple by design.

What is Halo Revival?

Halo Revival is an app for aesthetic planning, surgeon consult discovery, medspa search, and pre-consult organization.

Is it only for surgery?

No. Surgeons and medspas are separated, so surgical consults and non-surgical maintenance each have their own search flow.

Does it replace medical advice?

No. It helps organize research and booking steps. Treatment decisions should come from licensed clinicians.

Is my planning private?

The product is built around privacy-first planning. Sensitive keys and server-only data stay out of the public app bundle.
